Mission Files

01

CFD Analysis of NACA Airfoil

Performed 2D and 3D CFD analysis of NACA 2412 and NACA 4412 airfoil profiles using ANSYS Fluent. Studied lift, drag, and pressure coefficient distributions at various angles of attack. Validated numerical results against experimental wind tunnel data.

ANSYS Fluent CFD NACA Airfoil
02

Structural Analysis of Aircraft Wing

Conducted finite element analysis of a commercial aircraft wing under static and dynamic loading conditions using ANSYS Mechanical. Investigated stress distribution, deformation, and safety factors across multiple flight load cases.

ANSYS Mechanical FEA Structural Analysis
03

Design and Analysis of Winglet

Designed blended winglet geometries in CATIA V5 and analyzed their effect on induced drag reduction and aerodynamic efficiency. Compared multiple winglet configurations using CFD and validated performance improvements.

CATIA V5 CFD Winglet Design
04

Propulsion System Performance Analysis

Analyzed the thermodynamic performance of a turbofan engine cycle using MATLAB. Calculated specific thrust, TSFC, and overall efficiency across varying bypass ratios and flight conditions. Compared results with published engine performance data.

MATLAB Turbofan Propulsion
